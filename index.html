Rules:
Each round, the shotgun is loaded with a random mix of live and blank shells. Players take turns choosing to shoot themselves or the enemy. A live shell results in losing one heart, a blank shell allows you to gain another turn if shot at yourself. The objective is to deplete the enemy's hearts before losing all of yours.
